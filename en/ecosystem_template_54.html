V-Grip System

한국뉴매틱(VMECA)

Description
Picking up objects with different shapes, sizes, and porous materials can be challenging. The V-Grip, integrated with our highly efficient VMECA Vacuum Cartridge, addresses this issue effectively. Its flexible, soft, and durable foam can grip both even and uneven surfaces with ease. The V-Grip also features an adjustable check valve, allowing you to set it to off, low, medium, high, or max flow settings. Designed with a lightweight aluminum body frame, the V-Grip includes slots for easy installation and requires very low maintenance.

Specifications

> Handles various products with different shapes, sizes, and porous materials
> Flexible sealing foam (EPDM) sponge pad for excellent grip
> Adjustable check valve available
> Durable and lightweight aluminum body frame
> Easy installation and low maintenance
> Compatible with ISO robot tool flange (ISO 9409-1-50-4-M6)
